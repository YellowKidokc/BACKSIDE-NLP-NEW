Abstract

This paper introduces the Coherence Metric (χ), a quantitative measure of structural integrity derived from phase transition physics. We demonstrate that the same mathematical relationship governing critical phenomena in physical systems accurately describes decay patterns across nine independent social domains. The framework provides falsifiable predictions and measurement criteria independent of political interpretation.
