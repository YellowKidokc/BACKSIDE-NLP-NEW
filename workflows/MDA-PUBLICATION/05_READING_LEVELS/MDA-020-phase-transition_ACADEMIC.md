## The Coherence Factor (χ)  

**Definition — Coherence Factor (χ)** *(first use; social-system metric)*: A dimensionless scalar between 0 and 1 that quantifies the weighted average alignment of a civilization’s critical subsystems—families, institutions, trust networks, moral frameworks, and information flows—so that χ = 1 denotes perfect systemic alignment and χ = 0 denotes total fragmentation.  

χ = 1.0 → perfect alignment.  
χ = 0.0 → total fragmentation.  

Current U.S. χ: **≈ 0.27** [EMPIRICAL; Pew Research synthesis, 2024 [CITATION NEEDED: composite index]].  
Critical Threshold (χ_crit): **0.35** [STRUCTURAL; threshold derived from network-percolation theory [See: Watts, *Six Degrees*, 2003]].  
Status: **Threshold Crossed** [STRUCTURAL].

---

### Formalization of χ  

1.  Equation  
   \[
   χ \;=\; \frac{F + T + S + C + M + E + P + Σ + I}{9}
   \]

2.  Plain-English translation  
   χ equals the arithmetic mean of nine domain scores:  
   Family (F), Trust (T), Safety (S), Self-Control (C), Mental Health (M), Economic (E), Civic Participation (P), Shared Meaning (Σ), and Intergenerational Transfer (I).

3.  Predictive reading  
   • Increasing any single domain score raises χ proportionally;  
   • A simultaneous decline in multiple domains drives χ downward non-linearly once χ < χ_crit, triggering systemic reconfiguration (phase transition).

## Five Years  

Between 1968 and 1973, something broke.  
Not gradually. Not invisibly.  

**Everything. All at once.** [PROVISIONAL—“everything” used rhetorically; quantified domains shown below].  

In five years, coherence dropped from 0.72 to 0.55—a 24 % collapse [EMPIRICAL; Lowe dataset, 2024 [CITATION NEEDED: methods white-paper]].  

More decline in five years than in the previous sixty-five.  
This was not simple erosion; it was consistent with a **phase transition** *(first use; complex-systems term: an abrupt shift between system-level equilibria)*.

## The Physics of Phase Transition  

Water does not gradually become ice. At 33 °F it is liquid; at 32 °F it is solid.  
The thermodynamic phenomenon is termed **phase transition**—a discontinuous change in a system’s macro-state when a control parameter crosses a critical value [STRUCTURAL; classical thermodynamics [See: Callen, *Thermodynamics*, 1985]].  

Social systems exhibit analogous discontinuities [STRUCTURAL], a claim supported by percolation models of network fragility [See: Scheffer *et al.*, Nature, 2009].  

You can add pressure slowly, invisibly, for decades.  
The system absorbs it—until the final marginal perturbation precipitates collapse [STRUCTURAL].  

**1968-1973 constitutes America’s phase transition.**

## The Trigger Events  

### Assassinations (1963, 1968)  
JFK, Malcolm X, MLK, RFK—all shot within five years [EMPIRICAL].  

This did not merely remove individual leaders; it delegitimized reform via institutional channels [PROVISIONAL].  
**Coherence impact**: negative shock to institutional trust, civic hope, and future orientation.  

### Vietnam  
The Pentagon Papers (1971) exposed systematic misinformation [EMPIRICAL; Gravel Ed., 1971].  
**Coherence impact**: accelerated trust erosion; the deficit persists in longitudinal data through 2024 [EMPIRICAL; Pew, 2024].  

### Watergate  
Presidential criminality publicly verified, culminating in 1974 resignations [EMPIRICAL].  
**Coherence impact**: institutional trust reached a local minimum (~36 %) [EMPIRICAL; Gallup, 1974].  

### No-Fault Divorce  
California’s 1969 statute inaugurated nationwide legislative adoption by 1985 [EMPIRICAL; Allen, *No-Fault Divorce Reform*, 2002].  
**Coherence impact**: weakened family stability, the model’s smallest coherence node [STRUCTURAL].

### Sexual Revolution  
Widespread oral-contraceptive access after 1968 [EMPIRICAL; FDA approvals, 1960-1968].  
Traditional sexual-moral frameworks predicated on reproductive risk were destabilized [PROVISIONAL].  

### Church Collapse  
Mainline Protestant attendance fell 25–40 % between 1965 and 1975 [EMPIRICAL; Chaves, *American Religion*, 2011].  
**Coherence impact**: diminished production of shared meaning, civic volunteering, and cross-class interaction [STRUCTURAL].

## Why Everything Broke at Once  

**Inter-domain coupling** *(definition: mutual causal linkage among model domains)* implies that shocks propagate through feedback loops.  

Example causal chain [STRUCTURAL]:  
Trust ↓ ⇒ Civic Participation ↓ ⇒ Shared Meaning ↓ ⇒ Family Stability ↓ ⇒ Self-Control ↓ ⇒ Mental Health ↓ ⇒ Trust ↓↓.  

Once χ < χ_crit, the model predicts a **cascade** *(first use; systems term meaning self-reinforcing downward spiral)* that is difficult to arrest without external energy inputs proportional to lost coherence [STRUCTURAL; cf. Motter & Lai, PRL 2002].

## The Point of No Return  

χ = 0.55 (in 1973) remained above χ_crit = 0.35, so recovery was arithmetically possible [BOUNDARY].  
However, restorative institutions were themselves compromised, reducing feasible intervention sets [PROVISIONAL].
