### Definition of Key Framework Variable  
**The Coherence Factor (χ)**—*(symbol χ; from the Greek letter chi)*—is defined here as “the weighted mean of domain-specific integrity scores across a civilization’s critical subsystems” (family, trust, safety, etc.).  Formally:

1.  Equation  
   χ = (Σ wᵢ χᵢ) / (Σ wᵢ)

2.  Term-by-term translation  
   • χᵢ = coherence score of the i-th subsystem  
   • wᵢ = weight assigned to that subsystem (Σ wᵢ = 1 for normalization)

3.  Predictive behavior  
   • Increasing any χᵢ raises χ in direct proportion to its weight; systemic breakdown in a high-weight domain (e.g., family) exerts an outsized negative effect.
