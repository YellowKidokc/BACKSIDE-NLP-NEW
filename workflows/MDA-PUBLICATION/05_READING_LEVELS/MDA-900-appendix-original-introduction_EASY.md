# The Equation Nobody Wanted

## When a Nation Comes Unglued

**Coherence** is like a big puzzle where all the pieces lock tight and show one picture.  
America once had that tight puzzle. Churches were full, families steady, trust high.  
But when the puzzle pieces pop loose, disorder rushes in.

**Entropy (S)** is like a bedroom that gets messier every day you skip cleaning.  
**Grace (G)** is like fresh water poured on a wilting plant that lets it stand tall again.  
The story–version of the equation says:  
“If the water stays strong and the mess is small, the puzzle holds.  
If the mess explodes or the water stops, the puzzle collapses fast.”

I ran that story on America—and the numbers back it up.

## The Data No One Bothered to Gather

For eighteen months I read 1,300 reports and built one giant spreadsheet.  
Out of it grew **Theophysics**—like viewing the same mountain from both sides, one side physics, the other theology.  
Across three separate datasets the match was tighter than the proof for new particles in physics labs.

Then I pointed the method at the United States, 1900-2025.  
The result is one score I call the **Composite Coherence Index (CCI)**—like a school report card that rolls every subject into one grade.  
Peak years scored in the 90s. Today we hover near 20.  
That is not a slow slide. It is a **phase transition**, like calm water that suddenly roars into steam.

## The Five-Year Freefall

From 1968 to 1973 six different areas cracked at once.

Family split. No-fault divorce hit California; abortion became legal; divorce rates shot up.  
Trust dissolved. Vietnam, Watergate, and leaked secrets cut faith in government in half.  
Morals blurred. Hollywood ditched its old code; X-rated movies filled main theaters.  
Religion waned. Church attendance fell below 40 percent, and Christian identity started a long drop.  
Language roughened. Swear words and drug slang moved from alleys to prime-time TV.  
Money detached. The dollar left gold, and companies embraced throw-away products.

Six domains, five years, one synchronized break. That timing screams phase transition.

## What the Story Equation Says

Before 1968, grace poured in and entropy stayed in check.  
People tuned in, so the **observer function (O)**—like the share of folks wearing glasses so they can read the sign—was high.  
Then grace dipped, the mess spiked, and fewer people noticed the signal.  
The story warns: when O times G falls below a critical mess level, coherence doesn’t drift down—it snaps.  
After the snap, disorder feeds on the ruins and spreads itself.

That pattern matches the CCI graph.  
The steepest plunge, from 72 to 59, came in those same five years.
