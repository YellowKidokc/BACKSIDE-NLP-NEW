# The Coherence Metric

## Abstract

This paper introduces the **Coherence Metric** (χ), a quantitative measure of structural integrity derived from phase transition physics. We demonstrate that the same mathematical relationship governing critical phenomena in physical systems accurately describes decay patterns across nine independent social domains. Analysis of historical data from 1900–2025 reveals synchronized threshold-crossing events clustered within the **1968–1973 window**, suggesting a unified underlying mechanism rather than independent decay processes. The framework provides falsifiable predictions and measurement criteria independent of political interpretation.

## 8. Discussion

### The 1968–1973 Constraint Removal Cluster

Synchronized inflection across nine domains demands a common cause. The 1968–1973 window produced an identifiable cluster of constraint-removal legislative and cultural events. These are not interpretations. They are dates.

1965

**Griswold v. Connecticut** — Decoupled contraception from the state's interest in marriage, weakening the familial constraint structure.

1968

**Vietnam / Civil Unrest** — Institutional authority collapsed publicly. Trust in government began its terminal decline from 62% toward 16%.

1970

**No-Fault Divorce** — California's Family Law Act removed permanence from the marriage covenant. Divorce rate began vertical climb.

1971

**Gold Window Closure** — Nixon severed the dollar's constraint anchor. Currency decoupled from physical reality. $1.00 became $0.14 over 53 years.

1972

**Eisenstadt v. Baird** — Extended contraceptive access to unmarried individuals, completing the legal decoupling of sexuality from covenant.

1973

**Roe v. Wade** — Removed the final institutional constraint on the consequences of decoupled sexuality. Nine domains show synchronized inflection within this window.
