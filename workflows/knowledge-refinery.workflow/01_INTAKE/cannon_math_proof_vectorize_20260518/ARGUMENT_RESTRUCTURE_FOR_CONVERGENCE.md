## Recommended Argument Order

This order deliberately breaks the current story order. It should make the argument harder to dismiss.

It does not break the story. It makes one story visible at multiple levels:

```text
metaphysical question -> logical constraint -> physical analogue -> mathematical form -> Lean/proof target -> narrative/theological expression
```

1. **Boundary and honesty first.**
   State what is build-verified, what is internally canonical, what is conjectural, and what is external review pending. Use `docs/CLAIM_BOUNDARIES.md` as the guardrail.

2. **Define the formal object before the worldview.**
   Introduce Definition 10/11: ten typed factors, `S_eff`, product output `chi`, and the `C` versus `chi` distinction.

3. **Prove the product behavior.**
   Present zero-collapse, phase gate behavior, entropy attenuation, and positive-factor behavior from the clean Lean package. This is the machine-checkable base.

4. **Define the proof obligation for isomorphism.**
   Do not begin by claiming physics and theology are the same. Say: if two domains are structurally equivalent, they must preserve topology, collapse, boundary behavior, and relevant conservation.

5. **Show rejection-first controls.**
   Lead with false-positive tests. This builds trust: the framework has gates that can reject bad mappings.

6. **Introduce Law 4 as the first worked derivative family.**
   Use Strong Force / Love, fruits vector, anti-fruits, phase transition, and empirical metrics. Mark the Lean state honestly: rich model exists, complete physical derivation not yet formalized.

7. **Introduce Justice/Mercy as the second worked derivative family.**
   This should be elevated, not buried. It is one of the best arguments.

8. **Make the Justice/Mercy claim precise.**
   The core is not "God is nice and fair." The core is: justice and mercy are the same restoration operator, differing only by cost-bearer index `alpha`.

9. **Walk the five dimensions.**
   Proportionality, impartiality, truth-naming, victim restoration, cost bearer. This is where the argument becomes hard-edged.

10. **Run the anti-state test.**
   Anti-justice equals vengeance; anti-mercy equals enabling. This shows the operator predicts failure modes instead of merely naming virtues.

11. **Then bring in the Cross.**
   The Cross is not the opening move. It is the convergence solution after the operator is already constrained.

12. **State the Cross uniqueness claim carefully.**
   The current argument supports: judge-as-voluntary-cost-bearer is the unique configuration satisfying all five listed constraints. The next formal target is to encode those constraints in Lean.

13. **Connect Justice/Mercy back to Law 5.**
   The debt must be paid because disorder must be accounted for. Mercy moves the cost; it does not delete it.

14. **Then bring in Law 9.**
   Moral conservation should follow Justice/Mercy, not precede it. Once the debt/cost logic is clear, Law 9 explains why moral remainder cannot simply vanish.

15. **Position Noether as a target, not a completed proof.**
   Current material points toward moral conservation under time-translation symmetry, but the Lean layer still has assumptions. This is a prime next formalization target.

16. **Bring in Maxwell/Trinity only after the proof discipline is visible.**
   Use it as a rejection-first formal distinction, not as a rhetorical theology proof.

17. **Introduce empirical evidence last.**
   Evidence should support the formal spine; it should not carry the spine. Start with reproducible claims and controls.

18. **End with falsification conditions.**
   The strongest ending is not confidence; it is breakability. List what would break the framework.

## Full Derivation Chain Protocol

The goal is not to get one AI partner to agree. The goal is to run the whole chain from axioms to final claims and make every step survive independent re-derivation.

Each derivation step should have four tracks:

1. **Claim track:** the Theophysics claim being derived.
2. **Best rival track:** the strongest plausible alternative if the Theophysics claim is wrong.
3. **Failure track:** what exact error, contradiction, missing variable, false positive, or explanatory loss the rival produces.
4. **Survival track:** what the Theophysics claim preserves that the rivals cannot preserve.

This should be run by multiple AI partners independently. Convergence only counts if they reconstruct the same dependency order and locate the same weak links without being fed the conclusion.

### Convergence Rule

A step is stronger only when:

- at least two independent runs identify the same load-bearing dependency;
- the strongest wrong answer is not a strawman;
- the wrong answer fails for a named structural reason, not because it is disliked;
- the Theophysics answer preserves more constraints with fewer arbitrary patches;
- the claim boundary is explicit.
