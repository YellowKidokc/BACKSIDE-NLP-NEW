### TP-PRED-0001 - Lateral Rearrangement Without Reference Recovery

- UTC timestamp: 2026-05-19T00:31:00Z
- Exact claim: systems relying only on operations generated inside a deviation/error class will rearrange or displace failure but will not recover the reference state.
- Theorem / Lean dependency: `lean/ClosureTheorem.lean`, `no_error_closed_path_to_reference`.
- Measurable outcome: self-contained reform programs show substitution, vocabulary changes, temporary compliance, displacement of failure, or reversion under stress rather than durable reference alignment.
- Confirms it: longitudinal evidence that internally closed reform systems repeatedly produce lateral behavioral rearrangement without stable orientation change.
- Falsifies it: a self-contained reform system permanently restores reference alignment without external correction, accountability, grace/surrender, or outside reference input.
- Boundary: does not claim all behavior improvement is fake; it claims self-closed improvement is not reference-state recovery.
