## Core Distinction

The framework currently predicts most strongly in the **theological / human-system domain**, not in particle physics.

That is not a defect.

Physics is the highly formalized source domain. Theology, moral psychology, institutional behavior, and spiritual anthropology are the under-formalized target domains.

So the current prediction flow is:

```text
known physics/process structure -> predicted theology/human-system structure
```

The reverse flow:

```text
theological structure -> novel physics prediction
```

is not yet established except as a long-game possibility through the Lowe Coherence Lagrangian or future dimensional-reduction work.

## Prediction 1 - Lateral Rearrangement Without Reference Recovery

### Claim

Self-generated moral or institutional reform will produce movement between broken configurations more often than true reference-state recovery.

### T1 source

```text
Error-closed operations preserve Error.
Goldstone modes move along broken vacua, not back to the symmetric ground state.
```

### Observable domain

- addiction recovery without external accountability or surrender structure
- self-help movements
- institutional ethics reform
- purely internal corporate/church/academic reform
- moral education programs without external reference/correction

### Prediction

Programs relying only on internal self-correction should show:

1. behavioral substitution,
2. vocabulary changes,
3. temporary compliance,
4. displacement of failure into new forms,
5. reversion under stress,
6. no durable orientation change without external correction/coupling.

### Falsifier

Find a self-contained reform system that permanently restores reference alignment without external correction, accountability, grace/surrender, or outside reference input.

### Status

`TESTABLE HUMAN-DOMAIN PREDICTION`
