## Equation As Narrative

Use this compact dynamic form as the narrative bridge:

```text
dC/dt = O * G * (1 - C) - S * C
```

Where:

- `C` = coherence / Christ-aligned integration state
- `O` = openness / receptivity / posture
- `G` = grace / external source term
- `S` = sin / entropy / decoherence pressure
- `(1 - C)` = distance from full coherence, which determines the available restoration gradient
- `S * C` = corruption parasitically degrades prior order

This equation should not be treated as a replacement for the full ten-factor Master Equation. It is the narrative-dynamic reduction that lets the story be read as progression.

## Theological Refrain For Law, Blood, And Forgiveness

The Old Testament diagnostic layer should keep returning to this refrain:

```text
Before perfect holiness, every sin is categorically disqualifying.
Under forgiveness, sin is completely removed.
Within creaturely history, sins can still differ in consequence, knowledge, and responsibility.
```

Scripture anchors:

- James 2:10 - one breach makes the lawbreaker guilty before the whole law.
- Romans 3:23 and Romans 6:23 - all sin falls short and terminates in death.
- Psalm 103:12, Isaiah 43:25, Hebrews 8:12, and 1 John 1:9 - forgiveness removes sin completely.
- John 19:11 and Luke 12:47-48 - degrees of culpability and accountability remain real.

This keeps the theological layer aligned with the math layer: all sin shares the same structural category as deviation from perfect coherence, while consequence gradients still exist inside creaturely history.
