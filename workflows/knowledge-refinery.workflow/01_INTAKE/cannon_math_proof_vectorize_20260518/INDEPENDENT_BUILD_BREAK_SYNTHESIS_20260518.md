## Core Correction

The 7Q station did not show the paper is weak. It showed the paper is unlabeled.

The paper currently mixes scientific, formal, historical, theological, metaphysical, and narrative claims. When those lanes are unlabeled, the grader punishes `why` claims as if they were `how` claims and allows narrative force to masquerade as proof structure.

The repair is not to make theology pretend to be laboratory science. The repair is to label each claim lane, then score each lane by the right standard.

## Claim Labels

Use these labels before the next 7Q pass:

- `AUDITABLE-HOW`: mechanism, prediction, falsifier, boundary, measurable or formal test.
- `INTERPRETIVE-WHY`: meaning, teleology, Logos, theological purpose; scored for coherence and doctrinal fit, not lab proof.
- `BRIDGE-CLAIM`: asserts structural isomorphism between how-pattern and why-pattern; scored by mapping precision, alternative mappings, and failure modes.
- `HISTORICAL-WEIGHT`: raises or lowers posterior plausibility; does not compel metaphysical certainty.
- `NARRATIVE-REFRAIN`: rhetorical recurrence; useful for memory and persuasion, not counted as proof by itself.
