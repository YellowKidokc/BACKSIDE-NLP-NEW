# Story / Logic / Physics / Math Alignment

Run date: 2026-05-18

Purpose: keep the Theophysics derivation as one story told at different levels, not a pile of disconnected arguments.

## Core Pattern

Every section should progress through the same six-level stack:

```text
metaphysical question
-> logical constraint
-> physical/scientific analogue
-> mathematical form
-> Lean/proof target
-> narrative/theological expression
```

If the same story cannot be told across those levels, mark the section as incomplete.

## Justice/Mercy Placement

Justice/Mercy is not a side issue. It belongs in the center of the progression because it connects:

- Law 5 thermodynamic judgment;
- cost/debt/restoration logic;
- Law 9 moral conservation;
- the Cross as unique convergence;
- the transition from formal structure into gospel narrative.

It should come after fruits/coherence output and before Law 9/moral conservation.
