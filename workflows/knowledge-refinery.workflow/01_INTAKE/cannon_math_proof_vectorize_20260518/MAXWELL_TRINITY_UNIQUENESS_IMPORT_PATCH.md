Draft:

> If the candidate must be fully divine and fully human, how many persons can the candidate be? One? Two? Three? One person cannot be both source and mediator without collapsing relational distinction. Two persons can cooperate but do not yet supply a complete source-mediator-actualizer operation. Three persons with specific roles — source, mediator, actualizer — can interdepend in a way that is neither separable nor reducible.

Continue:

> This is not numerology. “Three” is not the point. The point is interdependence with irreducibility. Remove the source and nothing generates. Remove the mediator and nothing connects. Remove the actualizer and nothing completes. The unified operation cannot be reduced to one role or decomposed into arbitrary parts.

Physics bridge:

> Maxwell’s quaternion electromagnetism displays the same guarded structure: scalar source, vector field, unified product. Heaviside’s reduction was useful, but it obscured the deeper interdependence. The original structure preserves what reduction destroys.

Boundary:

> Two independent domains finding the same guarded triadic structure is evidence of structural signal, not proof by numerology.

## Honest Boundary

The Maxwell/Trinity Lean result should be stated carefully.

It proves:

- given the definitions, quaternion EM and Trinity satisfy the same triadic gate
- given the definitions, adversarial controls fail
- the controls are load-bearing
- removing guards permits false positives

It does not prove:

- Maxwell historically intended the theological structure
- the quaternion product is non-decomposable in every possible mathematical sense
- theology is uniquely forced by mathematics
- Christianity is proven by electromagnetism
