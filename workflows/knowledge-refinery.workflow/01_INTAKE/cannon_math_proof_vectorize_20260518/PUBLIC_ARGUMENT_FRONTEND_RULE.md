# Public Argument Frontend Rule
## Question-First Delivery, Math-Backed Structure

## Rule

Do not lead public-facing arguments with Trinity, Maxwell equations, master
equations, Lean, or dense axiomatic framing.

For family, Twitter, and general audiences, the front door must be hard,
self-defeating questions that ordinary people can feel before they can formally
analyze.

The math is the load-bearing structure underneath the floor. It is not the
first thing the listener sees when they walk in.

## Academic Exception

Keep the academic/technical formulation. Do not delete it, weaken it, or pretend
it is useless.

The academic version is for readers already willing to enter through formal
structure: mathematicians, physicists, philosophers, theologians, reviewers, and
AI partners doing proof/audit work.

The distinction is audience order, not truth order:

- **Public front door:** question first, human recognition first.
- **Academic front door:** definitions, equations, formal dependencies, proof
  boundaries.

Both are needed. They should cross-reference each other, but they should not be
merged into one opening move.

## Reason

If the argument begins with advanced physics or theological formalism, the
audience exits before the structural claim can land.

The goal is not to impress with abstraction. The goal is to ask a question so
precise that the listener's own answer exposes the dependency:

- "There is no truth." Is that true?
- "Morality is just opinion." Is that an opinion, or a rule?
- "Good people go to heaven." Which good people, by whose standard?
- "Just forgive everyone." Including the person who destroyed your child?
- "I need to understand before I obey." Would you accept that from a child at a cliff edge?

These are not gimmicks. They are first-principles access points.

## Layer Discipline

The public layer:

1. Ask the unavoidable question.
2. Ground it in human experience.
3. Let the listener feel the contradiction.
4. Only then show the structure.

The formal layer:

1. Keeps the argument honest.
2. Supplies mathematical pressure.
3. Guards against category errors.
4. Defines what is proven, bridged, or still open.

## Prohibited Front-Door Moves

- "The Trinity is encoded in Maxwell."
- "The master equation proves..."
- "Lean 4 verifies Christianity."
- "Quantum mechanics shows..."
- "Theophysics says..."

These may belong in academic papers, technical appendices, and formal proof
artifacts. They do not belong as the first sentence of the family/Twitter
version.

## Required Front-Door Move

Start with the question that makes denial unstable.

Then build:

question -> human lock -> metaphysical structure -> theology -> physics bridge
-> math skeleton -> objections.

## Short Form

Do not make people climb the scaffolding to enter the house.

Let them walk through the door.
