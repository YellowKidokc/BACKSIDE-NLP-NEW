# Speculative Metaphysics — Information, Soul, Coherence, And Falsifiability

Run context: 2026-05-18  
Status: speculative / private working note  
Public posture: do not publish as proof claim

## Why This Exists

These ideas are forming and should be preserved, but not presented publicly as proven physics, proven theology, or Lean-derived result.

This note is for continued exploration of the metaphysical bridge between:

- energy conservation
- information conservation
- consciousness / soul
- coherent identity
- Christ as final coherence
- falsifiability conditions

## Core Caution

Do not say:

```text
the soul is neutrinos
```

or:

```text
Hawking radiation proves eternal life
```

or:

```text
physics has proven the soul
```

Those are overclaims and will collapse under critique.

## Safer Working Claim

The physical product of labor decays. Biological life ends. Human memory distorts. Social reward systems vanish.

If anything persists beyond death, it is not ordinary material arrangement. The candidate category is coherent identity-information preserved in the final coherence structure of reality.

The theological claim is:

> God, and specifically Christ as final coherence, is the field/source in which coherent identity-information is preserved, judged, purified, and integrated.

This is a bridge claim, not yet a proof.

## Energy / Information Assumption

Working intuition:

- energy is transformed, not simply destroyed
- quantum theory strongly pressures information conservation
- black-hole physics makes this difficult through the information paradox
- Hawking radiation should be used as a pressure point, not a proof

Safer statement:

> Modern physics pressures the idea that information cannot simply vanish, even when the local form that carried it is destroyed.

Do not claim more than that unless a specific physics source/formal result is being cited.

## Neutrino Intuition

Neutrinos are useful as an analogy because they are:

- subtle
- weakly interacting
- difficult to detect
- capable of carrying energy/information
- not governed by ordinary visible-material intuition

Use neutrinos as:

```text
physical intuition for hidden weakly interacting carriers
```

Not as:

```text
identified soul-substance
```

## Work / Sweat Equity Claim

The deeper work claim is not about human appreciation or social memory.

It is ontological:

> Work only has enduring metaphysical persistence if the information generated by that work becomes coherent and integrated into the final structure of reality.

This avoids reducing the claim to:

```text
did another human value or remember the work?
```

The question is:

```text
does the act produce coherent information that survives ontological dissolution?
```

## Christ-Coherence Claim

Possible theological bridge:

> Christ is not merely the one who remembers coherent information. Christ is the final coherence in which all true information is held together.

Use caution:

- this is theological/metaphysical
- it should not be framed as proven physics
- it should be tested by Scripture, metaphysics, information theory, and formal coherence separately

## Falsifiability Pressure

Potential falsifier:

> Show that incoherent, non-integrated, or anti-coherent information persists with the same final status as coherent information.

Another falsifier:

> Show a final reality model where identity, moral truth, cost, intention, and consequence all persist without any unifying coherence field.

Another falsifier:

> Show that information conservation in physics has no legitimate bridge even analogically to identity, memory, moral cost, or personhood.
