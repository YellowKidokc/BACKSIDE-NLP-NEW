# THE LOWE FACTS FORMAT

> **Transparent Academic Papers Through Explicit Axioms**
> **The method does not guarantee truth. It guarantees transparency.**

---

## FACTS Summary

| Letter | Phase | Purpose | Key Question |
|--------|-------|---------|--------------|
| **F** | FIND | State the anomaly | What demands explanation |
| **A** | ADMIT | Disclose bias | Who is asking and why |
| **C** | CLAIM | Make thesis explicit | What exactly is being asserted |
| **T** | TEST | Show your work | How can this be checked |
| **S** | SNAP | Provide kill conditions | How can this be destroyed |

---

## Core Innovation

Forces **Biaxiosum** (explicit axiom declaration) rather than claimed objectivity with hidden assumptions.

Every paper must declare:
- Foundational axiom (if false, paper fails)
- Author worldview and priors
- What would change the author's mind
- Explicit falsification criteria
