# Reader Definition Bridge

Projection station for reader-facing definitions and analogies.

This station consumes `projection.academic.md`, `projection.academic.json`,
`paper-snapshot.json`, and `trace-ledger.json`. It emits a companion bridge:
terms above an 8th-grade level, plain definitions, equation explainers, and
analogies for recurring themes.

Failure mode to watch: letting an analogy become evidence. Analogies teach the
reader how to hold the concept; they do not prove the claim.
