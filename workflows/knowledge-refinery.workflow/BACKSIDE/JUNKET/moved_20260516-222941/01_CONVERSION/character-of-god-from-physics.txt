From the Weak Force: The Character of Mercy
Physics measures these properties
Governs radioactive decay — the transformation of identity
A neutron becomes a proton, an electron, and a neutrino
The transformation is irreversible under normal conditions
INSIDE the nucleus: the neutron is stable. Indefinitely.
OUTSIDE the nucleus: decays in 14 minutes and 39 seconds
The SAME particle. Different context. Completely different fate.
Read together
There is a force that governs how things lose their
original identity. The same particle that is stable forever inside
a bond decays in minutes when removed from it. The transformation
is real — what was one thing becomes something else entirely. But
the stability was always available. It was never taken away. It was
always there, inside the bond, waiting for the particle to stay.
It describes what happens when you leave the covenant. Not as
punishment. As physics. The same soul that is stable inside the
community of grace decays outside it. Not because God withdrew
protection. Because the strong force only holds you inside the
nucleus. Step outside, and the weak force does what it does.
The Mercy
The nucleus is always there. You can go back.
The bond re-forms. The stability returns. The decay stops.
A neutron that returns to the nucleus stops decaying immediately.
Physics calls it the weak force.
