# APO-02: Beginning of the Universe (Cosmological)
## "The universe began. Things that begin need a cause outside themselves."

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

The universe began — that's not theology, that's physics. The second law of thermodynamics guarantees it: entropy increases. If the universe were infinitely old, it would already be at maximum entropy. Heat death. But it isn't. So it started. And things that start need a cause that isn't themselves. What caused space, time, matter, and energy to exist? Not something made of space, time, matter, and energy.
