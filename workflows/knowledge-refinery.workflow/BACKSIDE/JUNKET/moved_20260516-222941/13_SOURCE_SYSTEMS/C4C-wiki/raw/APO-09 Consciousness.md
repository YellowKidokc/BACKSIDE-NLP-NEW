---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

The hard problem of consciousness isn't hard because we haven't solved it yet. It's hard because matter doesn't predict experience. Period. Your worldview has no explanation for the one thing you're using to evaluate everything.

Consciousness in the Theophysics framework is not an emergent property of complexity. It is co-fundamental — the C variable in the Master Equation. Coherence IS consciousness. It's not produced by matter. Matter is organized BY it.
