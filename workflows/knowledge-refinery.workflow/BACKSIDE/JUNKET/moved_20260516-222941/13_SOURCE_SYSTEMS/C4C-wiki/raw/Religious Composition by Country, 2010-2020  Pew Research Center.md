# Religious Composition by Country, 2010-2020

This interactive table shows the religious makeup of 201 countries and territories. Our accompanying report “[How the Global Religious Landscape Changed From 2010 to 2020](https://www.pewresearch.org/religion/2025/06/09/how-the-global-religious-landscape-changed-from-2010-to-2020/)” describes our [methodology](https://www.pewresearch.org/religion/2025/06/09/global-religious-change-methodology/) and key findings for every religious group and region. Estimates are based on more than 2,700 censuses and surveys. You can [also download the data](https://www.pewresearch.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/20/2025/06/Religious-Composition-2010-2020-dataset.zip).  

Refer to “[Religious Diversity Around the World](https://www.pewresearch.org/religion/2026/02/12/religious-diversity-around-the-world)” to learn how we [measure religious diversity](https://www.pewresearch.org/2026/02/12/methodology-religious-diversity-around-the-world/).  

Note: These religious composition numbers were originally published on June 9, 2025. On Feb. 12, 2026, religious diversity rankings were added to the table.
