## Definitions

**Debris Ratio** = Questions Opened / Questions Answered

- **< 1.0** = Theory resolves more than it creates (healthy)
- **1.0 - 3.0** = Theory creates as much as it resolves (stagnant)
- **> 3.0** = Theory creates more problems than it solves (toxic)
- **Infinite** = Theory creates problems with no path to resolution (terminal)

**Asks Why?**
- **YES** = Founders explicitly sought causal/ontological mechanisms
- **NO** = Founders explicitly forbade such questions
- **PARTIAL** = Mixed approach (some domains yes, others no)

**Unification Level**
- **UNIFIED** = Single interpretation dominates
- **CONTESTED** = 2-5 competing interpretations
- **FRAGMENTED** = 5+ interpretations, no convergence
