## THE 20 MOVES (ranked by TikTok frequency)

### MOVE 1 — HELL IS EVIL (115 hits — #1 by far)

"A loving God wouldn't torture people forever for finite sins." Sub-attacks: proportionality (finite sin → infinite punishment), character assassination ("your God is a monster"), the child question ("a 5-year-old in an unreached tribe?"), Christians weaponizing hell in debates.

### MOVE 2 — SLAVERY IN THE BIBLE (91 hits)

"Your Bible endorses owning people as property." Sub-attacks: Exodus 21 beating rules, Leviticus 25 foreign slaves as property, "the Bible never says slavery is wrong," comparing Biblical slavery to American chattel slavery.

### MOVE 3 — SCIENCE VS BIBLE (81 hits — 5× under-defended by Christians)

"Science has disproved the Bible" / "Evolution vs Genesis" / "We don't need God to explain anything." Sub-attacks: young earth creationism used as proxy for all Christianity, "science produces models not truth" reframe, God-of-the-gaps accusation, scientism as unexamined faith.

### MOVE 4 — RESURRECTION DIDN'T HAPPEN (62 hits)

"Dead people don't come back to life" / "extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence." Sub-attacks: naturalistic explanations (hallucination, stolen body, legend development), Paul vs Gospels timeline, comparison to other resurrection myths.

### MOVE 5 — BIBLE CONTRADICTS ITSELF (49 hits)

"The Bible is full of contradictions." Sub-attacks: creation account differences (Gen 1 vs Gen 2), genealogy discrepancies, differing resurrection accounts, numerical errors. NOTE: Christians over-defend this 3.5× relative to actual attack frequency.

### MOVE 6 — PROBLEM OF EVIL / SUFFERING (≈45 hits)

"If God is all-powerful and all-good, why does evil exist?" Sub-attacks: logical problem of evil (Epicurean formulation), evidential problem (quantity/distribution of suffering), animal suffering before the Fall, specific horrors (Holocaust, childhood cancer).

### MOVE 7 — GOD COMMITS GENOCIDE (≈40 hits)

"God ordered the slaughter of the Amalekites, Canaanites — women and children." Sub-attacks: direct divine commands to kill, Numbers 31 (Moses ordering killing of women and boys), 1 Samuel 15 (Saul and Agag), Joshua conquest narratives.

### MOVE 8 — MISOGYNY IN THE BIBLE (≈35 hits)

"The Bible treats women as property." Sub-attacks: "women be silent in church" (1 Cor 14:34), submission passages (Eph 5:22), Deuteronomy 22 rape laws, Lot offering his daughters, valuation differences in Leviticus 27.

### MOVE 9 — GOD IS EVIL / MORAL MONSTER (≈30 hits)

"The God of the Bible is the villain of his own story." Sub-attacks: combines hell + genocide + slavery + misogyny into composite character assassination. Dawkins' "jealous, petty, unjust" quote as rallying cry.

### MOVE 10 — BRONZE AGE VIOLENCE (≈28 hits)

"You're basing your life on a Bronze Age book written by goat herders." Sub-attacks: chronological snobbery, anti-intellectualism charge, comparison to other ancient myths dismissed as absurd.

### MOVE 11 — JESUS DIDN'T EXIST / MYTHICISM (≈25 hits)

"There's no contemporary evidence Jesus existed outside the Bible." Sub-attacks: no Roman records, Josephus interpolation claims, Paul never met Jesus, dying-and-rising god parallels (Osiris, Mithras, Dionysus).

### MOVE 12 — CHRISTIANS ARE HYPOCRITES (≈22 hits)

"Christians are the worst advertisement for Christianity." Sub-attacks: pastor scandals, political Christianity, "judge not" weaponized, prosperity gospel, church abuse cover-ups.

### MOVE 13 — LGBTQ: GOD HATES WHO I AM (≈20 hits)

"Your God made me this way then condemns me for it." Sub-attacks: born this way + created by God = trap, Leviticus 18/20 alongside ignored laws (shellfish, mixed fabrics), "love the sinner hate the sin" rejection, suicide rates among LGBTQ youth in religious families.

### MOVE 14 — EXCLUSIVITY / UNREACHED PEOPLES (≈18 hits)

"What about the billions who never heard of Jesus? They go to hell?" Sub-attacks: geographic lottery of birth, unfairness of soteriological exclusivism, comparison to other exclusive truth claims.

### MOVE 15 — FAITH = BELIEVING WITHOUT EVIDENCE (≈15 hits)

"Faith is just pretending to know things you don't know." Sub-attacks: Hebrews 11:1 misread as "faith = no evidence," science as the only valid epistemology, Boghossian's redefinition of faith, "I just go where the evidence leads."

### MOVE 16 — WHICH GOD? / RELIGIOUS PLURALISM (≈14 hits)

"There are thousands of gods. You reject all but one. I just go one god further." Sub-attacks: Stephen Roberts quote, genetic fallacy (you believe because of geography), all religions make exclusive claims so all cancel out.

### MOVE 17 — DIVINE HIDDENNESS (≈12 hits)

"If God existed and wanted a relationship, he'd make himself obvious." Sub-attacks: why doesn't God just appear, inconsistency between "God wants relationship" and "God hides," argument from reasonable nonbelief (Schellenberg).

### MOVE 18 — CHRISTIANS THREATENING HELL (≈10 hits)

"You're literally trying to scare me into believing." Note: This is Christians GENERATING atheist content. Atheist TikTok clips of Christians saying "you're going to hell" go viral. This is a self-inflicted wound.

### MOVE 19 — PERSONAL TRAGEDY (≈8 hits)

"My mom died of cancer. Where was your God?" Sub-attacks: personal experience trumps theological argument, "don't tell me it's God's plan," lived suffering vs abstract theodicy. NOTE: Emotionally the most dangerous — logic won't land here.

### MOVE 20 — CRUSADES / INQUISITION / HISTORY (≈6 hits)

"Religion has killed more people than anything in history." Sub-attacks: Crusades, Inquisition, witch trials, religious wars. NOTE: Historically this claim is false (20th century secular regimes killed far more) but it's emotionally sticky.
