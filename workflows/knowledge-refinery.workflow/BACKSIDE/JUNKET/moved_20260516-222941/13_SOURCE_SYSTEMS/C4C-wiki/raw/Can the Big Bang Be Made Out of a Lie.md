# Can the Big Bang Be Made Out of a Lie?

## The Question
Can the origin of the universe be the product of falsehood, randomness, or noise? Or does the information content of the Big Bang require a truthful source?

## The Information-Theoretic Answer: No.

### The Big Bang Is Maximum Information
The Big Bang is the most information-dense event in the history of the universe. Encoded in that initial singularity:
- Cosmological constant tuned to 1 part in 10^120
- Strong nuclear force tuned to within 0.5%
- Electromagnetic-to-gravitational force ratio: 1 in 10^40
- Every physical constant, every law, every particle ratio

This is not randomness. This is specified information at a density that dwarfs anything else in nature.

### Shannon's Theorem: Noise Cannot Generate Information
Claude Shannon proved that noise destroys information — it never creates it. The channel capacity theorem states: the output of a channel cannot contain more information than the input.

- A lie is noise (unpredictable, unstructured, entropic)
- Noise reduces signal, never increases it
- You cannot get MORE information out of noise than was in the signal

### The Second Law: Information Degrades
The second law of thermodynamics states entropy increases in closed systems. Information degrades over time. It does not spontaneously organize.

The universe has been LOSING information since the Big Bang. Every moment since the origin has been entropy increasing — stars burning out, structures decaying, information degrading.

The universe STARTED at peak information and has been losing it ever since.

### The Logical Chain

1. The Big Bang produced maximum information density
2. Noise/lies/randomness cannot generate information (Shannon)
3. Natural processes only degrade information (Second Law)
4. Therefore: the source of the Big Bang contained AT LEAST as much information as the Big Bang produced
5. The only thing that generates information without degrading it is truth (a deterministic, self-consistent signal)
6. Therefore: the source of the Big Bang is a truthful source
7. God identifies as truth itself (John 14:6)

### The Two Options

**Option A: Truthful Source**
The Big Bang was produced by something that could generate 10^120-level specified information. Truth is the only generator of information (Shannon). The source of the Big Bang is the source of truth. This is consistent with "In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth" (Gen 1:1) and "In the beginning was the Word (Logos)" (John 1:1) — Logos literally means structured information/reason.

The Big Bang emerged from nothing, by accident, randomly encoding fine-tuning at 10^120 precision. But "random" = maximum entropy = minimum information. You cannot get maximum information output from maximum entropy input. Shannon's theorem forbids it. This option violates information theory.
