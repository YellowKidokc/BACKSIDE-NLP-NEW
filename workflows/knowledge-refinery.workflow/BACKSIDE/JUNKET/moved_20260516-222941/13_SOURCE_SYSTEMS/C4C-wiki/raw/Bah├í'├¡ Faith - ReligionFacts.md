## Overview of Bahá'í Faith

Founded by Mirza Husayn-Ali (Baha'u'llah) in [Iran](https://religionfacts.com/iran) in 1852, the **Baha'i Faith** centers on a belief in one God and a goal of world peace and unity. Bahais acknowledge the prophets of major world religions as true messengers from God; Baha'u'llah is considered one of these prophets. Bahais are located worldwide but especially in Southeast Asia.
