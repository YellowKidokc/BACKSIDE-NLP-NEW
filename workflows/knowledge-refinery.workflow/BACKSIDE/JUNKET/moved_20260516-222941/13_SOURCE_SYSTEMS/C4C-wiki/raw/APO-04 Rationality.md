# APO-04: Rationality
## "If your brain is an accident, why trust it?"

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

If your brain is the product of unguided processes optimized for survival — not truth — then why should your reasoning be reliable? Evolution selects for fitness, not accuracy. A false belief that helps you survive is selected FOR. So every conclusion your brain reaches — including "there is no God" — was selected for survival value, not truth value. You're using an unreliable instrument to tell me reliable things. That's a problem.
