## The 5 Root Fallacies

1. **Decontextualization** (~40%) — context stripping, proof-texting
2. **Anachronism** (~20%) — chronological snobbery, modern morality applied backwards
3. **Equivocation** (~15%) — slippage on "genocide," "slavery," "command"
4. **False Framing** (~15%) — loaded question, strawman, false dichotomy
5. **Emotional Substitution** (~10%) — appeal to consequence, ad hominem

## ANE Comparison Killshots

**Slavery:** Hammurabi §16 = death for harboring runaway slave. Deut 23:15-16 = command to shelter runaways. 180° inversion.

**Genocide rhetoric:** Merneptah Stele (~1208 BC) — "Israel is laid waste, its seed is no more." Israel survived. Peer-era proof that "total destruction" = diplomatic hyperbole, not literal body count.
