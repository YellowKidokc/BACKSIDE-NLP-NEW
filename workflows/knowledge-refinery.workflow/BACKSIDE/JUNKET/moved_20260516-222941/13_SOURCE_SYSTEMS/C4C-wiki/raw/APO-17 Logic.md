# APO-17: Existence of Logic
## "Logic is universal, immaterial, and not physical. Where does it live?"

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

Logic isn't physical. You can't weigh the law of non-contradiction. You can't measure it with instruments. It's not located in space. But it's binding — everywhere, always, for everyone. It was true before humans existed. It'll be true after we're gone. On your worldview, only material things are real. So either logic isn't real — and everything you've ever argued is meaningless — or something immaterial IS real. And now you're in my territory.

---

## The Case (2-5 minutes)

Logic, like math (APO-05), is:
- Immaterial (not physical)
- Universal (true everywhere)
- Necessary (can't NOT be true)
- Eternal (pre-dates humans)
- Binding (you can't violate it)

This is the presuppositional siege. The atheist uses logic to attack theism — but logic is the theist's witness. They subpoenaed God's own evidence.

The framework gives this teeth: 6 Logos properties emerge as THEOREMS from CoherenceAlgebra (8 axioms). Simple, Consistent, True, Rational, Generative, Judging. These aren't declared — they're DERIVED. The logical structure of reality is a mathematical consequence of coherence algebra.

> "You just used logic against me. Logic is immaterial, universal, eternal, necessary, and binding. Your worldview says only material things are real. So you either just used something that doesn't exist on your view — or you believe in the immaterial. Welcome to my world."
