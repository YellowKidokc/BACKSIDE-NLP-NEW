## The Hook (30 seconds)

DNA isn't just a molecule. It's a four-letter code that stores, copies, and executes instructions. That's not chemistry — that's information processing. In every other domain of human experience, coded information comes from intelligence. Every time. No exceptions. And the information in one human cell would fill 1.5 gigabytes. The question isn't whether DNA is complex. It's why information — semantic, functional, executable information — exists in matter at all.
