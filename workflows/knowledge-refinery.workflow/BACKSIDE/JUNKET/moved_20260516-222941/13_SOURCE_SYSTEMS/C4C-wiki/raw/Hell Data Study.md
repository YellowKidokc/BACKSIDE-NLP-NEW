# Hell — What the Data Actually Says

## Jesus and Hell: The Numbers

| Metric | Count |
|--------|-------|
| Jesus hell-threat verses | 23 (excluding Mt 16:18 which is a promise) |
| Total words in those verses | 672 |
| Est. total Jesus words (KJV) | ~42,000 |
| **% of Jesus's speech that is hell-related** | **1.60%** |
| Jesus grace (charis) from His mouth | **0** |
| Kingdom of God/heaven mentions | 100+ (~4-5× more than hell) |

## Gospel Distribution

| Gospel | Hell verses | % of all hell content |
|--------|------------|----------------------|
| Matthew | 18 | **72%** |
| Mark | 3 | 12% |
| Luke | 4 | 16% |
| **John** | **0** | **0%** |

John — the gospel of "God so loved the world" — has **zero hell mentions** from Jesus.

## The Audience Finding (the killer stat)

| Audience | Verses | % |
|----------|--------|---|
| **Disciples (private)** | **11** | **47.8%** |
| Disciples + crowd | 4 | 17.4% |
| Crowd / cities | 4 | 17.4% |
| Pharisees (direct) | 3 | 13.0% |
| Pharisees + mixed | 1 | 4.3% |

**65.2% of Jesus's hell teaching was to his own disciples — private instruction to insiders.** Only ~17% was outsider-facing.

This kills the atheist frame "Jesus threatened unbelievers with eternal torment." He mostly warned his own followers about integrity and confronted religious hypocrites.

## Mt 25:41 — The Reframe

> "Depart from me, ye cursed, into everlasting fire, **prepared for the devil and his angels.**"

Hell is the ONLY destination Jesus identifies WHO it was made for — the devil and his angels, NOT humans. Humans who end up there aligned with the rebellion it was built for.

## Grace Study Cross-Reference

Jesus says grace (charis) zero times. He IS grace. The Paul-David symmetry: David owns 127/248 OT chesed (51.2%). Paul owns 88/137 NT charis (64.2%). Both murderers. Both forgiven. Both became the dominant grace voice in their testament.

First grace word: Genesis 6:8 (Noah). First NT grace word: Luke 1:30 (Mary). Same construction. Last word of the Bible: "The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all."
