# Key Defenders

Prominent scholars, philosophers, and apologists who have made significant contributions to the case for Christianity.

## Philosophers & Theologians
- **William Lane Craig** — Kalam cosmological argument, resurrection historicity, debates
- **Alvin Plantinga** — Reformed epistemology, evolutionary argument against naturalism
- **Richard Swinburne** — Probability-based arguments for God, miracles
- **N.T. Wright** — Historical resurrection argument, New Testament scholarship

## Historians & Biblical Scholars
- **Gary Habermas** — Minimal facts argument for the resurrection
- **Michael Licona** — Resurrection historiography
- **Richard Bauckham** — Eyewitness testimony in the Gospels
- **Craig Blomberg** — Historical reliability of the Gospels
- **Lydia McGrew** — Undesigned coincidences
- **Michael Brown** — Jewish objections to Jesus as Messiah

## Scientists & Science-Faith Scholars
- **John Lennox** — Mathematics professor, science-faith debates
- **Hugh Ross** — Astrophysicist, fine-tuning arguments
- **Stephen Meyer** — Intelligent design, information theory

## Popular Communicators
- **C.S. Lewis** — Mere Christianity, moral argument, argument from reason
- **Lee Strobel** — Investigative journalism approach to apologetics
- **Tim Keller** — Reason for God, cultural apologetics
