- **Uniformity of Nature:** The inductive leap that the future will behave like the past.
    
- **Moral Values:** The presupposition of honesty, integrity, and the "value" of truth-seeking.
    
- **The Existence of Numbers:** The ontological status of mathematical entities.
    
- **The Principle of Parsimony:** The unprovable aesthetic preference for simpler explanations.
