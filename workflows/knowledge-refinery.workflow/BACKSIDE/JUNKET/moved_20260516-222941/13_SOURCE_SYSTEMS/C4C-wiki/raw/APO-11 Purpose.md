# APO-11: Purpose & Meaning
## "Evolution doesn't require meaning. Yet you deeply pursue it. Where did that come from?"

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

Rocks don't ask why they exist. Animals don't write philosophy. But you — you can't stop asking. Why am I here? What's the point? Does any of this matter? Evolution doesn't produce that question. Survival doesn't require it. An organism that just eats, reproduces, and dies is perfectly fit. The fact that you need MORE than survival — that you need MEANING — is a signal that meaning exists. You don't get hungry in a world with no food.
