# The Epistemic Architecture of Science
## What Science Actually Produces — According to Its Own Practitioners

### THE MOVE
When an atheist says "show me empirical evidence" — they're standing on a platform they think is bedrock. It isn't. Their own philosophers, their own physicists, their own history says so.

Science does NOT produce permanent truth. It produces **a convergent sequence of models** that are increasingly successful at predicting phenomena. Every single "settled" theory in history has been replaced. Their own people call this the **pessimistic meta-induction** (Laudan): because nearly every successful theory of the past has been proven false, we have no inductive grounds to believe current theories will escape the same fate.

### THEIR OWN PEOPLE SAY IT

**Niels Bohr:** "What we observe is not nature itself, but nature exposed to our method of questioning."

**Richard Feynman:** "Science is the belief in the ignorance of experts." Called science a "system of guesses" and a "game."

**Stephen Hawking:** Championed "model-dependent realism" — there is NO theory-independent concept of reality. Truth is an illusion; all we have are "networks of world pictures."

**Karl Popper:** No number of positive observations can EVER definitively confirm a universal law. Science produces "corroborated" theories — hypotheses that haven't been disproven YET.

**Thomas Kuhn:** Science does NOT draw ontologically "closer to truth." It produces "instrumental improvement" — better problem-solving, not better truth.
