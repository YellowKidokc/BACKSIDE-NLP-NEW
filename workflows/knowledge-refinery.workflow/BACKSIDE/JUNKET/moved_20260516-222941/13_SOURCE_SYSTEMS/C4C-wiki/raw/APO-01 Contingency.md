# APO-01: Contingency
## "Everything that exists depends on something else — except one thing."

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

Everything you see depends on something else. Your chair depends on wood. Wood depends on trees. Trees depend on soil, water, sunlight. Follow any chain long enough and you either hit something that doesn't depend on anything — or you have an infinite regress, which means nothing is actually explained. A necessary being that depends on nothing. That's the definition of God.
