# Objections and Hard Questions

## Purpose
This section doesn't follow the FOR/AGAINST structure. These are the questions that make believers uncomfortable — and that's exactly why they need to be here. Burying hard questions makes the vault dishonest. Surfacing them builds trust with contributors who aren't already convinced.

## The Questions

- **[[Problem of Evil (Deep Dive)]]** — Why does God allow suffering, especially of innocents?
- **[[Old Testament Violence & Genocide Commands]]** — How do you reconcile a loving God with commands to destroy entire peoples?
- **[[Hell & Eternal Punishment]]** — Is infinite punishment for finite sin just?
- **[[Exclusivism & Religious Pluralism]]** — What about the billions who never heard?
- **[[Slavery in the Bible]]** — Why didn't God simply condemn slavery?
- **[[Gender & Sexuality in Scripture]]** — Are biblical teachings on gender and sexuality defensible?
- **[[Unanswered Prayer]]** — Why does prayer appear to have no measurable effect?
- **[[Hypocrisy of the Church]]** — Does institutional failure invalidate the message?

## How to Use This Section
Each note should honestly present the objection at full strength, then explore the strongest available responses. If no satisfying response exists, say so. That's more useful than a bad answer.
