## Rules of Engagement for Live Apologetics Rooms

## RULE ZERO: THE FUNDAMENTAL ASYMMETRY

**You are not on defense. You have never been on defense.**

The atheist position requires: no God, no objective morality, no grounding for logic, no explanation for consciousness, no account of fine-tuning, no source of information, no grounding for math, and no explanation for why anything exists.

The Christian position requires: one hypothesis (God) that resolves all of the above.

**They owe nine explanations. You owe one. Act like it.**

## THE SEVEN LAWS OF TIKTOK COMBAT

### LAW 1: OFFENSE FIRST, ALWAYS

The default state of TikTok apologetics is defense: "Prove God exists." "Where's your evidence?" "The Bible says X." You're always responding, always justifying, always on the back foot.

**Flip it.** Your opening move is ALWAYS a claim, not a response. Front-load your strongest material before they can set the frame. Whoever sets the frame controls the room.

**Execution:**
- Open with your claim stack (see [[TIKTOK_ARGUMENT_STRUCTURE]])
- Do NOT pause for their reaction between claims
- Stack 3-5 claims in rapid succession
- THEN stop and let them respond
- Now THEY are responding to YOU

**The principle:** The person asking questions controls the conversation. The person answering questions is on defense. Never answer. Always ask.


### LAW 2: NEVER DEFEND YOUR CLAIM — EXPAND IT

When they attack your claim, the instinct is to defend it. Resist. Instead, add MORE claims on top. Force them to attack a growing pile, not a single point.

**What this looks like:**

You: "Math is immaterial, universal, eternal, necessary, and non-deceptive. Name one other thing with those properties."
Them: "Math is just a human tool."
You (WRONG): "No it isn't, here's why..."
You (RIGHT): "OK — so if math is man-made, then science is built on a subjective foundation. Which means every empirical claim you've ever made is built on something you invented. AND the universe ran on mathematical structure for 13.8 billion years before humans existed. AND your brain uses math to tell me math is man-made. So you're either wrong, or you just destroyed your own epistemology. Pick one."

**The principle:** Don't defend — EXPAND. Make the pile bigger. They have to deal with ALL of it, not just one point.


### LAW 3: STEAL THE FLOOR

The most devastating move in TikTok apologetics is demonstrating that their tools belong to you.

Logic, math, truth, moral evaluation, the uniformity of nature — these are the infrastructure of rational thought. They are ALL presuppositions that science cannot prove. They exist in YOUR domain, not theirs.

**The deployment:**

When they use logic against you: "That's interesting — where did logic come from in your worldview? Because it's not material. You can't weigh it. Can't measure it. It's universal and necessary. Sound familiar?"

When they use math against you: "You just used math to make your point. Math is immaterial, eternal, and universal. Your worldview says only material things are real. So either math isn't real — and your argument just collapsed — or something immaterial IS real, and you owe me an explanation for where it lives."

When they invoke morality: "You just said my position is WRONG. That's a moral/truth claim. Where does 'wrong' live in a universe of particles and forces? Show me the wrongon. Point to the justice quark."

**The kill line:** "You can't use those things against me because you can't validate those things. And if you can't validate them, you can't use them to invalidate me. But I CAN validate them — they're grounded in Logos. So every time you reason, every time you calculate, every time you evaluate — you're borrowing from my framework. You're standing on my floor to tell me my floor doesn't exist."
