# Key Critics

Prominent scholars, philosophers, and public intellectuals who have made significant contributions challenging Christianity. Understanding their arguments at full strength is essential for honest engagement.

## Philosophers
- **David Hume** — Classic argument against miracles, problem of induction
- **J.L. Mackie** — Logical problem of evil
- **William Rowe** — Evidential problem of evil
- **Graham Oppy** — Arguments against natural theology
- **J.H. Schellenberg** — Divine hiddenness argument

## Biblical Scholars & Historians
- **Bart Ehrman** — Textual criticism, misquoting Jesus, historical Jesus skepticism
- **Richard Carrier** — Mythicist position, Bayesian analysis
- **Robert Price** — Mythicist position, New Testament parallels to myth
- **John Dominic Crossan** — Historical Jesus minimalism
- **Hector Avalos** — Biblical morality critique

## Scientists
- **Richard Dawkins** — The God Delusion, evolutionary critique of design
- **Lawrence Krauss** — Universe from nothing, cosmological arguments
- **Sam Harris** — Moral landscape without God, neuroscience of belief
- **Daniel Dennett** — Consciousness as computation, religion as natural phenomenon

## Public Intellectuals
- **Christopher Hitchens** — God Is Not Great, moral critique of religion
- **Matt Dillahunty** — Debate-focused atheist advocacy
- **Alex O'Connor (CosmicSkeptic)** — Philosophical objections to theism
