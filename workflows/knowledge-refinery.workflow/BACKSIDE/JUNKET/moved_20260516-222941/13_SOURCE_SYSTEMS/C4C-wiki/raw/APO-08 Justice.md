# APO-08: Justice
## "You demand justice. Where does that demand live in a universe of particles?"

## The Hook (30 seconds)

You demand justice. Not just for yourself — for strangers. For people across the ocean you'll never meet. That demand has zero survival value. It often gets people killed. Evolution produces "avoid danger." It does NOT produce "that's unfair." So where did your justice instinct come from? Show me the justice quark. Point to the fairness particle. You can't — because justice isn't physical. But it's more real to you than gravity.
