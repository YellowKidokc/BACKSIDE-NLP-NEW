## Domain 1: Thermodynamic Singularity (Penrose Number)

The initial entropy of the universe requires precision of 1 part in 10^(10^123).

- 10^123 is already larger than atoms in the universe (10^80)
- The Penrose Number is 1 followed by 10^123 zeros
- Even writing a zero on every proton/neutron in the observable universe falls trillions of orders of magnitude short
- Inflation doesn't solve it — the inflaton field must be EVEN MORE finely tuned
