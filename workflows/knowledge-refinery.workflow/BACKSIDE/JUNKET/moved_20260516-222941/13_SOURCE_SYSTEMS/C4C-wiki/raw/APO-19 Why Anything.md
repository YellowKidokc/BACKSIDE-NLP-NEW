# APO-19: Why Is There Something Rather Than Nothing?
## "Nothing should exist. But something does. That needs explanation beyond itself."

---

## The Hook (30 seconds)

Why does anything exist? Not "what caused the Big Bang" — WHY IS THERE A "WHAT" AT ALL? Physics describes what exists and how it behaves. It has never, in all of history, told you why there's a "what" in the first place. "Nothing" is simpler. "Nothing" requires no explanation. But here we are. Something exists. And that something demands an explanation that isn't itself.
