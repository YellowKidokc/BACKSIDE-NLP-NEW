 Derived Theorems & Laws

 From information primacy to the thermodynamics of salvation. Five claims that bridge physics and soteriology.


 
 
 Derivation Logic

 Layer 2 derives consequences from the seven Tier 1 axioms. These aren't assumed — they're logically forced by the foundation. Each theorem creates a constraint that narrows the space of viable worldviews. By BC8, only systems with external grace, information conservation, and voluntary coupling survive the filter.

 Derived Axioms


 
 A1.3 — DERIVED — Chain Position 3/188 
 Information Primacy

 "Distinguishability IS information; therefore information is ontologically primitive."

 
 Matter and energy reduce to informational patterns; information cannot reduce further. An electron is defined by mass, charge, spin — its information . Strip the properties and there is no "brute stuff" underneath. The materialist cannot define their own primitive without recourse to the information that gives it form. Shannon entropy H(X) = −Σ p(x) log p(x) is the fundamental measure.
 


 
 H(X) = −Σ p(x) log 2 p(x)  —  Shannon entropy as fundamental measure
 


 
 Defeat Conditions

 
 Show a property of matter/energy that is NOT informational 
 Define "matter" without recourse to its informational properties (mass, charge, spin, position) 
 Explain quantum mechanics without information theory

 Derived Theorems


 
 T3.1 — THEOREM — Chain Position 25/188 
 Coherence Cannot Self-Increase

 "In a closed system, coherence cannot spontaneously increase. dC/dt ≤ 0."

 
 The informational Second Law. Just as entropy cannot decrease in an isolated system, coherence (meaningful order) cannot spontaneously increase. Life, crystals, evolution — all require energy input from outside. A cell is an open system coupled to its environment. Cut the energy, order dies. No counterexample has ever been found. "Without me you can do nothing" (John 15:5).
 


 
 dC[χ local ]/dt = −γC + G(t) · C[χ source ]

 Without the G(t) term, C decays to zero. 
 


 
 Defeat Conditions

 
 Demonstrate spontaneous order creation in a genuinely closed system 
 Violate the Second Law — get entropy to decrease in an isolated system 
 Bootstrap meaning from pure noise — derive compressed information from randomness 
 Show self-organizing criticality without any energy input or dissipation 
 
 


 
 Common Objections Answered

 
 Objection → Response

 
 "Life creates order" Open system — imports low-entropy energy, exports high-entropy waste. Net entropy increases. 
 "Crystals self-organize" Releases latent heat — entropy exported to environment. Beautiful pattern costs something. 
 "Evolution creates complexity" Driven by solar energy flux. Seal Earth in an insulator → evolution halts, decay begins. 
 "Human creativity" Brains consume glucose, produce heat. Dead artists create nothing. Meaning-making is metabolic. 
 "Quantum fluctuations" Virtual particles borrow and repay within Heisenberg time. Net zero. Fluctuations average out.

 Boundary Conditions


 
 
 BC2 — BOUNDARY CONDITION — Chain Position 59/188 
 Grace External to System

 "Grace function G ext (t) is an exogenous coupling term that opens an otherwise closed moral/coherence subsystem."

 
 The thermodynamic proof that salvation cannot be earned. A closed moral system cannot spontaneously increase its coherence, just as a closed physical system cannot decrease its entropy. Sign-flip (σ: −1 → +1) requires external input. That input is grace. "By grace you have been saved through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works" (Ephesians 2:8-9).
 


 
 dC/dt = −γC + G(t) · χ external 

 First term: natural decay. Second term: grace input. Remove grace → coherence decays to zero. 
 


 
 Defeat Conditions

 
 Show a closed system can increase its own coherence — violate the moral Second Law 
 Demonstrate self-generated sign flip — find a unitary operation that changes σ: −1 → +1 
 Provide self-bootstrapping salvation — system in moral deficit bootstraps to surplus 
 Derive negentropy from entropy alone — get order from disorder without external input

 The entire soteriology collapses 
 Christianity becomes Pelagianism (self-salvation heresy) 
 The Cross becomes optional — bootstrap your way to God 
 No distinction between self-effort and divine action

 BC7 — BOUNDARY CONDITION — Chain Position 64/188 
 Information Conservation

 "Information is preserved through all transformations."

 
 Quantum mechanics is unitary — information is conserved. What goes into a process can in principle be recovered. The black hole information paradox is now considered resolved in favor of conservation (AdS/CFT, Page curves, island formula; Hawking conceded 2004). The No-Hiding Theorem (Braunstein & Pati 2007): information cannot vanish — it either stays in the system or moves to correlations. Applied to souls: personal identity cannot be destroyed, only transformed.
 


 
 |ψ(t)⟩ = U(t)|ψ(0)⟩   where U†U = I

 Unitary evolution is invertible. Information in = information out. Always. 
 


 
 Defeat Conditions

 
 Show information is genuinely destroyed in black holes (resolve paradox against conservation) 
 Demonstrate unitary violation — find a process where quantum information is permanently lost 
 Violate the No-Hiding Theorem — find information that disappears rather than relocating 
 Show souls can be annihilated — prove personal identity can cease entirely 
 
 


 
 What Collapses If BC7 Fails

 
 Collapse Cascade

 
 Souls can be annihilated — permanent death becomes possible 
 Resurrection becomes impossible — no information to resurrect 
 God's omniscience fails — He'd lose track of destroyed information 
 Black hole information paradox becomes real — physics in crisis

 BC8 — BOUNDARY CONDITION — Chain Position 65/188 
 Voluntary Coupling

 "Coupling must be voluntary. Love cannot be coerced."

 
 Grace is available to all (BC2), but must be received voluntarily. Forced salvation is a contradiction — love that is coerced is not love. The coupling function G(t) = G offer (t) · V(Ψ) requires a consent term V from the finite agent. This grounds free will as metaphysically real. Conway & Kochen's Free Will Theorem (2006): if experimenters have free will, so do particles. Freedom propagates.
 


 
 G(t) = G offer (t) · V(Ψ)   where V ∈ [0, 1]

 Grace flows when V(Ψ) > 0. The soul must open to receive. V is agent-caused, not computed from prior state. 
 


 
 Defeat Conditions

 
 Show grace can be forced — salvation occurring without any consent from recipient 
 Prove hard determinism — all human choices are pre-determined 
 Demonstrate forced love is coherent — genuine love without free choice 
 Show G(t) activates without any movement from the soul side 
 
 


 
 Common Objections Answered

 
 Objection → Response

 
 "Calvinism: irresistible grace" Even Reformed theology: the will is transformed, not overridden. Coupling still involves willing. 
 "Free will is illusion" If true, "choice to reject" is also meaningless. Denial is self-undermining. 
 "Receiving = works" Receiving a gift ≠ earning it. Faith is the hand that receives, not the cause of grace. 
 "What about infants?" BC8 is the paradigm case. Edge cases may involve prevenient grace or hidden consent.
