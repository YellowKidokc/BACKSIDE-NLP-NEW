**A Note Before We Begin**

Nothing in this article reinterprets Scripture. I take Genesis at its word — there was a garden, there was a tree, there was a choice, and everything changed. What I'm doing is asking: what does that event look like from inside a physics equation? Not to modernize an ancient story but because I believe the ancient story described something real — something so real that the laws of physics still carry the fingerprint of it. If Genesis is true, then physics and Genesis are describing the same event. They should agree.

They do.
