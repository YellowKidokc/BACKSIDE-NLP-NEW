What these scores measure
Structural properties of the text. High coherence = all ten Master Equation variables engaged. High truth density = claims paired with evidence at a high ratio. Neither score validates content. That remains the reader’s work.
What these scores do not measure
Correctness of conclusions, theological validity, experimental replicability, or domain-specific peer review. This is a structural audit, not a truth oracle.
Reproducibility Guarantee

This pipeline is deterministic. Given identical input, it produces identical output across runs, machines, and operators.
