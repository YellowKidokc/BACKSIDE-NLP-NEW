## 2. THE PRE-FALL / FALL / RESTORATION SEQUENCE

$$E_{\text{total}} = E_{\text{ordered}} + E_{\text{disordered}} \quad \text{where} \quad E_{\text{disordered}} = 0$$

$$E_{\text{total}} = E_{\text{ordered}}' + E_{\text{disordered}}' \quad \text{where} \quad E_{\text{disordered}}' > 0$$

$$E_{\text{disordered}} \xrightarrow{\text{Grace}} E_{\text{ordered}}$$

The conversion cost is real (Landauer's principle — erasing one bit of disorder costs kT ln 2 of real energy). The conversion energy comes from an external, inexhaustible source (Holy Spirit / Law 2). The conversion mechanism is gravitational (Grace / Law 1) — pulling scattered structure back toward center.
