# The Day Time Began

**A Note Before We Begin**  
This article treats the Bible and science as two ways of describing the same reality. When Genesis says God created the heavens and earth, that is taken literally. When physics describes the universe’s beginning and laws, that is also taken literally. The question is not whether they conflict, but what it means that they agree. This article explores where time itself begins, finding the same story in both the Hebrew text and physics equations.

> "For dust you are, and to dust you shall return." — Genesis 3:19  
> This is not a moral judgment but a physics statement. It describes a process: dust becomes living being and returns to dust. This shows sequence, irreversibility, and the arrow of time.

Before God spoke these words, there was no "before." No ticking clock, no entropy pushing matter toward disorder, no death scheduled. The main physics equation describing reality had no time term. The system was perfectly coherent and static—not frozen, but complete. Nothing changed because there was no time.

Previous articles showed Eden as a quantum superposition—a perfectly coherent state that collapsed when Adam measured it by eating the fruit. The will survived but entered a noisy, degraded state. This article goes deeper: the collapse did not happen within time; it created time. The first wavefunction collapse is the first tick of the clock. And we have data to support this.

## Before the Bite: A Universe Without Time

The main physics equation describing reality integrates over space and time. But before the Fall, there was no time dimension. The equation reduced to space only. Coherence was a field over space, not changing over time.

What does this mean physically?

No time means no sequence. Events don’t have an order because ordering requires time. The question “what happened first?” has no answer—not because the record is lost, but because time itself does not exist.

No time means no entropy. The second law of thermodynamics says entropy increases over time. Without time, entropy has no direction. Coherence does not degrade because there is no temporal direction for degradation.

No time means no death. Death is a process of decay through a sequence of states. Without sequence, there is no process. “You shall surely die” is not a threat about what will happen next. It is a statement about what happens when time appears: mortality becomes possible because sequence exists.

> Note: Satan’s fall was spatial, not temporal. His corruption has no time dimension, so it has no past tense. He just is fallen permanently. This is why angels have no redemption offer—the mechanism requires before and after, which time provides.

## The Bite: When Time Appears

Adam eats. The quantum state collapses. In that collapse, something unprecedented happens—a new dimension of reality opens: time.

The bite introduces matter-knowledge coupling. Adam now knows good and evil—information acquired through physical interaction. This awareness brings vulnerability, decay, and the possibility of loss. That awareness is the arrow of time. Knowing a state can degrade means knowing sequence exists—because degradation is a process requiring before and after.

The chain is:

1. Collapse produces definite states (good and evil known, not just potential).  
2. Definite states produce distinguishable conditions (naked vs. clothed, innocent vs. guilty).  
3. Distinguishable conditions allow transitions between them.  
4. Transitions require before and after.  
5. Before and after is time.

The first collapse is the first moment of time—not metaphorically, but literally. Time did not exist before; the Fall generated time.

After the bite, the main physics equation activates fully, integrating over space and time. Entropy begins to drain coherence. Coherence starts a long decline. Death becomes a process that begins and will complete unless interrupted.

“For dust you are, and to dust you shall return” is the first statement about temporal physics in the Bible. It describes a trajectory with a start, middle, and end. That trajectory requires time. God tells Adam: you now live in a dimension that has an end.

## The Trinity at the First Collapse

All three Persons of the Trinity are present at the moment of collapse. This is not incidental. The framework predicts it—Trinity Actualization requires three irreducible operations, and the first collapse exhibits all three.

- **Father: The Boundary.** God sets the law—“You shall not eat of it.” This boundary defines what is permitted and prohibited. In quantum terms, the Father provides the full set of possible states. Without this boundary, there is no superposition to collapse.

- **Son: The Logos Field.** “In the beginning was the Word.” The Logos maintains order and intelligibility through the collapse. Without Logos, collapse produces chaos—random outcomes with no pattern or meaning. The Son selects the outcome, ensuring the world retains structure and covenant possibility.

- **Spirit: The Witness.** “They heard the sound of the LORD God walking in the garden.” The Spirit actualizes the potential into reality, making the collapse real. The Spirit does not set the boundary or select the outcome but makes it happen.

These three functions occur simultaneously in one event, producing one irreversible outcome. The first wavefunction collapse is the first Trinity Actualization in time.

Two regimes stand out:

### Pre-Flood: Flat Lifespans

Pre-Flood average lifespan is about 912 years with little variation and no significant decline over more than a thousand years. This suggests that although time and entropy exist, their effect on humans was weak. Humans were in time but barely affected by entropy. Residual coherence from the pre-Fall state was still very high.

### The Flood: Phase Transition

After the Flood, lifespans drop sharply from around 950 to 600 years in one generation. This suggests a physical change increased humanity’s coupling to entropy, accelerating decay. The Flood’s description of “fountains of the great deep burst open” may represent environmental or genetic changes that altered this coupling.

### Post-Flood: Exponential Decay

Post-Flood lifespans follow a textbook exponential decay curve typical of quantum decoherence. The data fits this model well, explaining nearly 89% of the variation. The decay approaches a floor, a minimum lifespan that coherence cannot fall below.

One notable outlier is **Eber**, whose lifespan is significantly above the predicted decay curve. Eber is the ancestor of the Hebrews and the covenant line, suggesting a re-coherence effect—something adding coherence beyond entropy’s pull.

## The Floor Is Grace

If decoherence continued unchecked, lifespans would drop to zero and humanity would go extinct. But they don’t. The decay curve has a floor.

The floor is explained by a term representing **grace**—God’s sustaining, negentropic input that prevents total decay. Even when humanity is weak and entropy is strong, grace keeps coherence above zero.

The model’s floor corresponds to about 93 years, Moses’ Psalm 90:10 says about 70 years, and modern lifespans average around 78 years. All agree there is a lower bound on human coherence that entropy cannot breach. This floor is grace, hidden in plain sight in Genesis genealogies.

This is a prophecy in future tense. Prophecy requires sequence—a “not yet” and a “will be.” Redemption requires a “was lost, now found.” None of this is possible without time.

Genesis 3:15 is spoken after the collapse—after time exists. It is the first prophecy and only possible because the first collapse created the temporal framework prophecy depends on.

Satan saw the knowledge transfer but did not anticipate that the tree would open time itself—a trapdoor for redemption disguised as a consequence.

The Cross was planned before creation but could only be executed in a frame where before and after exist. The Fall created that frame. Time is not the enemy; time is the medium of redemption.
