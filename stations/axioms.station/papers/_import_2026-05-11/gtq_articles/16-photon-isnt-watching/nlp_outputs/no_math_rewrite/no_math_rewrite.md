# Why the Photon Isn't Watching You Back

**A Note Before We Begin**  
The double-slit experiment is famous in quantum physics. It shows that particles like electrons behave differently depending on whether someone is observing them. This is not just a metaphor; it is part of the actual physics and laboratory results. The universe seems to "check" if it is being watched before deciding how to behave.

This article argues that consciousness is not outside physics but part of it. It is not claiming psychic powers or magic, just that the state of the observer’s consciousness influences quantum outcomes in a small but measurable way. If true, this means consciousness is inside the equations of physics, changing how we understand both science and ancient texts like Genesis.
