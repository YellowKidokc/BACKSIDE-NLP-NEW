# Dynamics (D)

Relational process; the active flow between and within forms that creates change and connection.

## Definition

Dynamics is the relational aspect of reality - the flow, change, connection, and process that links forms together and animates them. Where Information is source and Form is structure, Dynamics is the living relationship between them.

---

## Trinity Connection
**Aspect:** Spirit (Relationship)

The Spirit is the dynamic bond between Father and Son - the living love that flows between them. The Spirit is not static but ever-moving, ever-connecting, ever-animating. This is why the Spirit is associated with wind, fire, water - all dynamic phenomena.

---

## Physics <-> Theology Bridge
**Physics side:** Force, energy transfer, field dynamics, wave propagation, time evolution

**Theology side:** The movement of the Spirit, divine action in history, sanctification as process

**The bridge:** Every physical force and dynamic process is an expression of the Spirit's characteristic - the connecting, animating, transforming power that relates things to one another.

---

## Key Insight

Without Dynamics, Information and Form would be frozen and dead. The Spirit brings life precisely by introducing relationship and change. This is why "the letter kills but the Spirit gives life" - static form without dynamic relationship is death.

---

## Mathematical Expression

$$
D = \frac{\partial F}{\partial t}
$$

Dynamics as the time evolution of Form - how structure changes and relates across time.
