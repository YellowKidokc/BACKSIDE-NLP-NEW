# Grace (G)

Negentropic divine action; the ordering force that opposes entropy and restores coherence.

## Definition

Grace is the active, sustaining power of God that counteracts decay (entropy) and moves systems toward greater order, coherence, and life. In theophysics, Grace functions as the fundamental negentropic force in the universe.

---

## Trinity Connection
**Aspect:** Triune (all three persons active)

- **Father:** Source of Grace - the wellspring from which ordering power flows
- **Son:** Channel of Grace - the Logos through which Grace takes form
- **Spirit:** Application of Grace - the dynamic presence that applies Grace to creation

---

## Physics <-> Theology Bridge
**Physics side:** Negentropy - the tendency toward order; information preservation; anti-entropic processes

**Theology side:** Divine favor and sustaining power; unmerited gift that restores and orders

**The bridge:** Grace IS negentropy at the theological level - the same ordering principle described in different vocabularies. What physics calls "local decreases in entropy," theology calls "the work of Grace."

---

## Key Insight

Grace is not merely forgiveness or favor - it is the active force that holds reality together against dissolution. Every ordered structure, every preserved information pattern, every instance of life persisting against decay is Grace in action.

---

## Mathematical Expression

$$
G = -\frac{dS}{dt} \cdot \chi
$$

Where G (Grace) is proportional to the rate of entropy decrease, modulated by chi (the Logos field).
