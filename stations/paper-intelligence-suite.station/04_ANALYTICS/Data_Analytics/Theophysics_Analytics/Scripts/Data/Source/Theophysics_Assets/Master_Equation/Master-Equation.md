## 
The [[Theophysics_Glossary#Master Equation|Master Equation]], or **Trinitarian Tensor**, describes the minimal field structure of reality:

\[
\chi \;=\; \rho_{\text{info}} \;\otimes\; \psi \;\otimes\; G
\]

- \(\rho_{\text{info}}\): The **Information Substrate** (coherence density), associated with The Father.
- \(\psi\): The **[[Theophysics_Glossary#Witness Field|Witness Field]]** (observation amplitude), associated with The Holy Spirit.
- \(G\): The **[[Theophysics_Glossary#Grace Function|Grace Function]]** (feedback/correction magnitude), associated with The Son.

Each of the 11 Laws is a different projection of this single [[Theophysics_Glossary#Master Equation|Master Equation]] onto a specific domain (informational, observational, moral, etc.).
