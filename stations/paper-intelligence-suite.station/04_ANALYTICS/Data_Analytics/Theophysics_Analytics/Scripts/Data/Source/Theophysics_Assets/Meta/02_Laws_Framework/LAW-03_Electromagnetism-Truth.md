## Statement of the Law

**Divine Truth functions as a self-propagating electromagnetic field. It originates from a source (The [[Theophysics_Glossary#Logos|Logos]]), is composed of indivisible quanta (revelations), has no isolated fragments (is perfectly self-consistent), and propagates at a universal constant speed, carrying energy and information that shapes the spiritual landscape.**

### Formal Expression (Maxwell's Equations of Truth)
The dynamics of this "Truth-Field" directly parallel Maxwell's equations, which govern light itself.

1.  **Gauss’s Law (Electric):** `∇ · E = ρ/ε₀`
    -   *Physics:* Electric fields originate from charges.
    -   *Spiritual Parallel:* Truth radiates from a source—a "charge" like Scripture or a divine statement. *"The word goes out..."*

2.  **Gauss’s Law (Magnetic):** `∇ · B = 0`
    -   *Physics:* There are no magnetic monopoles; field lines are always closed loops.
    -   *Spiritual Parallel:* Truth cannot be broken into isolated fragments. It is a holistic, self-consistent whole. *"Scripture cannot be broken."*

3.  **Faraday’s Law:** `∇ × E = -∂B/∂t`
    -   *Physics:* A changing magnetic field induces an electric field.
    -   *Spiritual Parallel:* Living, dynamic Truth (internalized Scripture) creates new currents of understanding and fresh revelation in a believer's life.

4.  **Ampère-Maxwell Law:** `∇ × B = μ₀J + μ₀ε₀∂E/∂t`
    -   *Physics:* Electric currents and changing electric fields create magnetic fields.
    -   *Spiritual Parallel:* Truth put into action (discipleship, teaching) sustains and expands the field of influence for others, strengthening coherence.

## Model (Molecule Hub)

### The Wave Nature of Truth
Derived from Maxwell's equations, the electromagnetic wave equation shows that light propagates inevitably at a constant speed, `c`.
-   **Spiritual Parallel:** Truth also propagates at a universal constant. It cannot be stopped or slowed, only blocked from perception by a receiver.

### Wave-Particle Duality
Light exhibits both wave-like (pervasive, field-like) and particle-like (localized, photons) properties.
-   **Wave Truth:** Universal, eternal principles of God.
-   **Particle Truth:** Specific, personal revelations—a single verse that lands with power, a direct encounter with Christ.

### Interference and Coherence
Light waves can interfere constructively (amplifying each other) or destructively (canceling each other out).
-   **Spiritual Parallel:** When truths resonate, they create spiritual **coherence** and amplify their impact. When lies and distortions are introduced, they create destructive interference, obscuring or canceling the signal.

## Trinity Connection

-   **The Father (The Source):** He is the ultimate "charge" (ρ) from which the field of Truth originates.
-   **The Son (The Field):** Jesus is the embodiment of the field itself—the [[Theophysics_Glossary#Logos|Logos]] made manifest. He is the radiance.
-   **The Spirit (The Propagation):** The Holy Spirit is the dynamic force that propagates the field, inducing new understanding and sustaining its coherence through believers.
