## TRADITIONAL DEFINITIONS

### Physics Definition
**Standard Meaning**: A measure of the correlation between the phases of wave systems - the ability of waves to maintain a fixed relationship in space and time, enabling interference patterns.

**Example Usage**: 
> "Quantum coherence refers to the property of quantum systems to exist in superposition states with definite phase relationships between component states."

**Mathematical Expression**:
```
C(τ) = ⟨E(t)E*(t+τ)⟩ / ⟨|E(t)|²⟩
(First-order coherence function)
```

### Theology Definition
**Standard Meaning**: Logical consistency and internal agreement within a system of doctrine - the absence of contradiction in biblical interpretation and systematic theology.

**Example Usage**:
> "The coherence of Scripture refers to the unity and harmony of biblical teaching across all 66 books despite multiple human authors."

**Scriptural Reference**:
- 2 Timothy 3:16 - "All Scripture is God-breathed"
- John 10:35 - "Scripture cannot be broken"

### Philosophy Definition
**Standard Meaning**: A theory of truth stating that a belief is true if it coheres (fits logically) with other accepted beliefs in a system.

**Key Thinkers**: Spinoza, Hegel, Bradley, Neurath
