# Information (I)

Pattern distinction; the fundamental substrate of reality that precedes matter and energy.

## Definition

Information is the capacity to distinguish one state from another - the fundamental "difference that makes a difference" (Bateson). In theophysics, information is ontologically primary: matter and energy are expressions of underlying informational patterns.

---

## Trinity Connection
**Aspect:** Father (Source)

The Father is the source of all information - the originating pattern from which all distinction flows. "In the beginning" implies the first distinction, the first information. The Father knows all because all information originates in Him.

---

## Physics <-> Theology Bridge
**Physics side:** Shannon information, quantum information, bits and qubits, pattern preservation

**Theology side:** Divine knowledge, the mind of God, the original pattern/blueprint of creation

**The bridge:** What physics measures as "information" is the footprint of divine knowledge in the physical realm. The laws of physics are compressed expressions of infinite divine information.

---

## Key Insight

Information cannot be created or destroyed - only transformed. This mirrors the theological truth that God's knowledge is eternal and complete. What we call "new information" is really the unfolding revelation of what was always known.

---

## Mathematical Expression

$$
I = -\sum_i p_i \log_2 p_i
$$

Shannon entropy - the measure of information content in a system.
