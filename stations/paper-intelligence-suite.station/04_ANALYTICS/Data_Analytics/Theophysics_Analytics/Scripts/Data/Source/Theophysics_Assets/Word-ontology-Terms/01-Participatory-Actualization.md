## JUSTIFICATION

### Why This Term Exists
The "Measurement Problem" terminology creates three false assumptions:
1. That observation is a **problem** rather than the primary mechanism
2. That the observer is **passive** rather than participatory
3. That collapse is **random** rather than purposeful co-creation

**Participatory Actualization** reframes the phenomenon as:
- **The central feature** of quantum mechanics (not a bug)
- **Co-creative partnership** between consciousness and Logos Field
- **Purposeful selection** from potential to actual

- **Quantum Potential** → exists in superposition
- **Conscious Observation** → introduces boundary condition

**Participatory Actualization** embeds the Trinity into the quantum measurement process.
