# Resurrection (R)

Complete entropy reversal; the restoration of full coherence after dissolution.

## Definition

Resurrection is the ultimate negentropic event - the complete reversal of entropy and the restoration of coherence beyond the original state. It is not merely returning to a previous condition but achieving a higher-order integration that incorporates and transcends the dissolution.

---

## Trinity Connection
**Aspect:** Triune (all three persons)

- **Father:** The will and power that resurrects - source of life that cannot be extinguished
- **Son:** The pattern of resurrection - first to rise, demonstrating the path
- **Spirit:** The agent of resurrection - "the Spirit of him who raised Jesus from the dead"

---

## Physics <-> Theology Bridge
**Physics side:** Maximum entropy reversal, spontaneous reordering, information recovery from apparent loss

**Theology side:** Bodily resurrection, new creation, restoration of all things (Acts 3:21)

**The bridge:** Resurrection reveals that entropy is not ultimate. The thermodynamic arrow can be reversed by sufficient Grace. What appears as permanent information loss in physics is revealed as recoverable under divine action.

---

## Key Insight

Resurrection is the proof that Grace > Entropy. If Grace could not ultimately overcome entropy, there would be no resurrection. The resurrection of Christ is the empirical demonstration that negentropy can win completely and permanently.

---

## Mathematical Expression

$$
R = \lim_{G \to \infty} (C_{final} - C_{initial}) > 0
$$

Resurrection occurs when Grace is sufficient to produce net positive coherence gain, even after apparent total dissolution.
