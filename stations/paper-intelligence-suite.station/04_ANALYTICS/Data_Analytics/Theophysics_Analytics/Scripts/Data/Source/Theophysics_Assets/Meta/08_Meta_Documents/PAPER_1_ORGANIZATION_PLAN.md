## 🎯 **ARCHITECT'S BLUEPRINT SUMMARY**

**Core Thesis:** General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics are not two separate theories but are two different phenomenological descriptions of a single, underlying, conscious, and informational substrate (The Logos Field, χ).

**Mathematical Claim:** The framework does not require new mathematical structures but reinterprets existing ones. **Empirical Claim:** The framework is supported by the experimentally verified reality of the "participatory universe" (e.g., the delayed-choice experiment), which standard physics acknowledges but fails to explain at an ontological level.
