*   **[[Theophysics_Glossary#Logos|Logos]] as Ordering Principle:** The introduction of the _Logos_ as the "universal, immanent organizing principle" provides the essential mechanism for coherence in an information-based reality, addressing the critical question of what governs the actualization process.

### 3. Resolution of GR/QM Schism and [[Theophysics_Glossary#measurement problem|Measurement Problem]]

The proposed framework offers an elegant and comprehensive resolution to the fundamental incompatibilities between GR and QM, particularly the [[Theophysics_Glossary#measurement problem|measurement problem]] and the role of the observer.

*   **Determinism vs. Probability Reframed:** The framework clarifies that the evolution of potentiality (QFT) is deterministic, while actualization (measurement) is probabilistic, seeing them as "two phases of a single cycle."
*   **Observer Paradox Dissolved:** The universe is "self-observing and self-creating." The "collapse" is a fundamental process of information actualization, with consciousness as a "coherent manifestation of the universe's capacity for self-perception." This circumvents the need for a mysterious external observer.
