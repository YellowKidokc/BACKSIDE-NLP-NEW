## Core Thesis / Belief Statement  
The Logos Framework stands or falls by falsifiability.  
This paper translates its metaphysical propositions into **empirical protocols** — a series of coherence, resonance, and informational-field experiments designed to verify or refute the existence of χ-coupling in physical and cognitive domains.

## Mathematical Framework / Key Equation  

$$
\Huge
E_{\text{obs}} = \int_V \chi(x,t)\, \psi_S(x,t)\, dV
$$

The observed coherence energy \(E_{\text{obs}}\) integrates Logos potential χ with soul-field ψ_S over a bounded volume V.  
Deviations between predicted and measured \(E_{\text{obs}}\) define the falsification metric ΔE.

## Empirical / Falsifiable Claim  
Three experimental tiers:  
1. **Quantum-Optic Tier:** delayed-choice photon interferometry under observer-coherence control.  
2. **Biophysical Tier:** heart-brain-phase coherence vs. local field potential entropy.  
3. **Collective Tier:** group-coherence experiments testing non-local synchronization.  

Any tier producing ΔE ≈ 0 supports χ-field presence; persistent ΔE ≫ 0 falsifies the model.
