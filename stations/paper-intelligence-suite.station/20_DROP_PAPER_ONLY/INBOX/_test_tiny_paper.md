# Tiny Test Paper

## Thesis
Information conservation is the deepest constraint on physical law.

## Argument
If information is preserved across physical processes, then the second law
of thermodynamics emerges as a coarse-grained projection. We claim that the
unitarity of quantum mechanics already encodes this principle.

## Equation
S = -k_B ∑ p_i log p_i

## Falsifiability
Any observation of genuine information loss in a closed system would refute
the framework.
