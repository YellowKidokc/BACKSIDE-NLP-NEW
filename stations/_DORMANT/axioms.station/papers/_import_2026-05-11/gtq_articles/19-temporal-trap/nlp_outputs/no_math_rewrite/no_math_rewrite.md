# The Temporal Trap

Paul says that the rulers of this age didn’t understand what they were doing when they crucified Jesus. Satan, who should have stopped the Cross, actually caused it because he misunderstood time itself.

Time is not just a neutral backdrop. It was created at the Fall and has a direction—like an arrow pointing forward. Satan helped bring time into existence but never fully understood how it works.

This article explains the full strategy: from Satan’s fall as a spatial event, to his mistake about time, to how the Cross’s meaning is determined by a future event (the Resurrection) that changes the past.
