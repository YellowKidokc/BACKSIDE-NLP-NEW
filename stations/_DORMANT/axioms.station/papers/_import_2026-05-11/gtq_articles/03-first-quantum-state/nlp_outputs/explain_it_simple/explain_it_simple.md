# Explain It Simple

- **The 2-minute version:**  
This article says the story in the Bible about the first choice humans made in the Garden of Eden can be seen as a special moment in physics called a "quantum state." It means that the Bible and science might be telling the same story about how everything changed at the start of human history.

- **Key terms in plain language:**  
  - *Genesis:* The first book of the Bible, telling the story of creation and the first humans.  
  - *Quantum state:* A way physics describes the smallest parts of reality, like atoms and particles, which can be in different conditions.  
  - *Superposition:* When something in quantum physics can be in multiple states at once until observed.  
  - *Isomorphism:* A perfect match or similarity between two different things.  

- **What this is really saying:**  
The moment Adam and Eve made their choice in the garden is not just a story but a real event that changed the universe. Physics, especially quantum physics, can describe this change because the laws of nature still show signs of it. So, science and the Bible are connected, not opposed.

- **What would prove it wrong, if present:**  
If it could be shown that the biblical story is purely symbolic with no real event behind it, or if quantum physics could never be linked to free will or moral choice, then this idea would fail. Also, if experiments disproved any mind-matter connection, that would challenge the claim.
