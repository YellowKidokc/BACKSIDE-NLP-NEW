Transparency Notice

This paper has been evaluated by the Theophysics Paper Intelligence pipeline — a deterministic, open-source scoring system distributed as a Docker container. The pipeline performs automated analysis across multiple dimensions including coherence index (χ), truth density, claim-support ratio, equation coverage, lexical and semantic Fruits alignment, readability metrics, and Master Equation variable mapping.

What these scores measure
Structural properties of the text. High coherence = all ten Master Equation variables engaged. High truth density = claims paired with evidence at a high ratio. Neither score validates content. That remains the reader’s work.
What these scores do not measure
Correctness of conclusions, theological validity, experimental replicability, or domain-specific peer review. This is a structural audit, not a truth oracle.
Reproducibility Guarantee

This pipeline is deterministic. Given identical input, it produces identical output across runs, machines, and operators. The Docker image, source code, and scoring methodology are publicly available. You are invited—encouraged—to run this pipeline against this paper, against any paper in this corpus, or against any paper from any source.

  • Counterargument detection identifies explicit rebuttal language only; structural objections embedded in argument flow may not be counted.
