# The Equation

$$\frac{dC}{dt} = O \cdot G(1-C) - S \cdot C$$
      
      C = coherence with God (0 to 1)  •  O = openness (human reception)  •  G = grace (sovereign, external)  •  S = entropy/sin (always pulling downward)
      The surrender parameter s ranges from −1 (fully closed) to +1 (fully surrendered). The coupling function α(s) = (1+s)/2 determines how much of G actually gets through.
      At s = −1 (spiritually dead): coupling = zero. The equation reduces to pure decay: dC/dt = −S·C. This is MacArthur’s “dead in trespasses and sins.” The math proves what he preaches.
      Phase 1 — God moves s from −1 toward 0. Effectual calling. Irresistible grace. G acts on the closed system to open it. The person at s = −1 did not choose this. Dead things don’t choose. This is monergism — salvation initiated by God alone.
      Phase 2 — The person moves s from 0 toward +1. The channel is open. Grace is flowing. The growth term — O·G(1−C) — requires BOTH God’s input AND human openness. Neither alone produces growth. This is where “bow down, repent, believe, submit” lives.
