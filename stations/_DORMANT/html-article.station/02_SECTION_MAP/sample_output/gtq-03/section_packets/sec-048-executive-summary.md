— Core Thesis
        The coherence equation $dC/dt = O \cdot G(1-C) - S \cdot C$ dissolves the Calvinist–Arminian debate not by picking a side but by showing that both camps have identified one real variable in a two-variable equation. Grace $G$ is real, externally sourced, and universally available. Openness $O$ is real, under some form of agent influence, and genuinely modulates the coupling. The fight about which term is more important is rendered meaningless by the multiplication structure.
