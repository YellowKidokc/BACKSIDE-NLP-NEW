# Checklist

<!-- section_id: sec-002-checklist -->
<!-- heading_path: Pilot Pre-Flight Checklist > Checklist -->

1. Confirm aircraft identity and flight number.
2. Verify fuel level.
3. Inspect engine status.
4. Confirm navigation instruments.
5. Test radio communication.
6. Confirm weather clearance.
7. Verify runway assignment.
8. Confirm crew readiness.
9. Review emergency procedures.
10. Authorize takeoff only after all checks pass.
