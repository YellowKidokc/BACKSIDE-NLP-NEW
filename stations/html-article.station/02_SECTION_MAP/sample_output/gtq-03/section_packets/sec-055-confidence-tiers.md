Load-Bearing — Bet On This
        The equation structure is motivated, not invented. The logistic growth form with external drive and decay term is standard across multiple scientific domains. We are applying a known dynamical structure to a new domain, not creating exotic mathematics.
      
      
        Load-Bearing
        Philippians 2:12–13 structurally requires both terms. Paul's simultaneous "work out" and "God works in" is not theological hedge. It is simultaneous assertion. Any single-variable model contradicts the text.
      
      
        Load-Bearing
        The three-pathway neuroscience mapping uses established findings. DMN hyperactivation in self-referential states, PFC overactivation in rule-following, parasympathetic elevation in surrender — these are documented findings applied to the framework, not invented for it.
      

      
        Suggestive — Needs More Work
        The mapping of $G$ to grace and $O$ to openness is interpretive. The argument for these mappings is motivated but not uniquely forced. Alternative parameterizations deserve testing.
      
      
        Suggestive
        The $s$ parameter requires an operationalization protocol. Theoretical construct is sound; measurement pathway is not yet established. The framework's testability depends on solving this.
      
      
        Suggestive
        D2 receptor restoration in Path 3 transitions is preliminary. The claim is consistent with existing literature on addiction recovery and meditation research, but a direct link to the surrender parameter in religious contexts has not been established.
      

      
        Overextended — Acknowledged
        The claim to "dissolve" the Calvinist–Arminian debate is a reframing, not a refutation. Both camps can respond that we have redefined their terms. The reframing is useful for testability; its theological authority is a separate question we do not fully answer.
      
      
        Overextended
        The equation does not address double predestination. Whether God actively sets $G = 0$ for the non-elect, and on what basis, is not resolved by the equation. We acknowledged this in the Audit; it is worth repeating here as a genuine gap.
      
      
        Overextended
        Conviction of voice throughout. The paper is written with confident tone for readability. Confidence is not proof. This rigor tab is where we pay for that choice.
