# The Structural Answer

<!-- section_id: sec-084-the-structural-answer -->
<!-- heading_path: Free Will in Two Frames > Why Did God Drown Everybody? > The Structural Answer -->

Eden was the baseline. Direct Spirit coupling. Full protection. Humanity built for that state.
      The Fall broke the protection layer. Direct adversarial access became possible. The entire Old Testament is the damaged period.
      The Cross clears the debt blocking Spirit distribution. “It is finished” means the blocking term is paid. The distribution mechanism is now clear.
      Pentecost is the restoration, not the innovation. It reinstates the protection layer that Eden had. The OT God isn’t the “real God showing his true colors” with the NT God softening with age. The OT is God operating in emergency mode during a crisis that wasn’t supposed to last forever.
