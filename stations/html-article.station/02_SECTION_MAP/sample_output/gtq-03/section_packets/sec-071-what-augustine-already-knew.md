# What Augustine Already Knew

<!-- section_id: sec-071-what-augustine-already-knew -->
<!-- heading_path: Free Will in Two Frames > MacArthur and the Equation > What Augustine Already Knew -->

Twelve centuries before this equation, Augustine mapped four states of the human will:
      
        
          
            
              State
              Augustine
              Equation
            
          
          
            
              Before the Fall
              Able to sin, able not to sin
              Full freedom. Channel open. Entropy not yet accumulated.
            
            
              After the Fall
              Unable not to sin
              s = −1. Coupling zero. Only the decay term running.
            
            
              Under Grace
              Able not to sin
              Channel reopened. Phase 2 active. Growth possible.
            
            
              In Glory
              Unable to sin
              s = +1. Perfect coupling. Will and God produce identical output.
            
          
        
      
      At s = +1, the “freedom” to choose sin was always the freedom to choose decay. Losing it isn’t losing freedom. It’s gaining it. Paul said it: “It is no longer I who live, but Christ who lives in me” (Galatians 2:20).
