# MacArthur and the Equation

<!-- section_id: sec-062-macarthur-and-the-equation -->
<!-- heading_path: Free Will in Two Frames > MacArthur and the Equation -->

The 1,600-year debate dissolved in one line of math.

      
        I have deep respect for John MacArthur and the Reformed tradition. This article doesn’t critique his theology — it formalizes it. When MacArthur says “dead in sin,” that maps to a specific value in a specific equation. When he says “God grants repentance,” that maps to a specific operation. When he calls sovereignty and responsibility an “antinomy” — two truths that seem contradictory but aren’t — I believe he’s exactly right. They aren’t contradictory. They’re two phases of one process.
